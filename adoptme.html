Adopt Me!

About:

Adopt me is a game that revolves around adopting and caring for pets. Pets hatch from eggs, different eggs hatch different pets. Some pets can ONLY be purchased by robux.

Origins:

Released on July, 13, 2017 by Uplift Games

Trivia:
