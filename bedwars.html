Bedwars

About:

Bedwars is a team/solo strategy game where players battle each other, and (as the name suggests) break each others bed and kill them. After destroying the opponents bed, the team needs to defeat all the remaining opponents. U can buy different kits to give u special abilities. Most costs robux.

Origins:

Released on May, 28, 2021 by Easy.gg

Trivia:

  1. Did u know, the famous Youtuber, Tanqr, used to play Bedwars ALOT?

Personal Opinion:

This game is very good. It is a skill based game, and if you dont play solos, teamwork is important!
