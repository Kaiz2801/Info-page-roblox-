Blox Fruits

About:

Blox fruits is a grinding game where players can eat specdial fruits to gain supernatural powers, explore vast lands, and become powerful pirates or marines.

Origins:

Released on June, 8, 2019 by mygame43 and rip_indra

Trivia:

  1. Did u know, blox fruits was called blox piece when it was in beta stage?
  2. Blox fruits is based on an anime show, called One piece?

Personal Opinion:

This game is great. Although getting permanent fruits costs alot of robux(5000 robux for when the creator is writing this), it is a game of patience, skill and determination.
